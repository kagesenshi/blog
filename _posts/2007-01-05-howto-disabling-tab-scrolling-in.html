--- layout: post title: 'HOWTO: Disabling tab scrolling in Firefox2' date: '2007-01-05T19:59:00.000+08:00' author: izhar firdaus tags: - Open Source - Hacks - HOWTO modified_time: '2008-01-11T11:26:05.250+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-28371165.post-4438363992645439368 blogger_orig_url: https://blog.kagesenshi.org/2007/01/howto-disabling-tab-scrolling-in.html --- Firefox 2 introduces tab scrolling feature similar to Epiphany and several other browsers. However, while this feature seems to help stop tabs from becoming too small and hide its title, its considered annoying to some users. Not be able to see all of the tabs in once glance might not be preferable to some.

To disable this, goto about:config and set this
browser.tabs.tabMinWidth = 0
