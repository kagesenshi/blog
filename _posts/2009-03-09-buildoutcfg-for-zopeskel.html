--- layout: post title: Buildout.cfg for ZopeSkel date: '2009-03-09T02:42:00.002+08:00' author: izhar firdaus tags: - Python - Plone - Zope modified_time: '2009-03-14T09:05:23.919+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-28371165.post-3641409656502157868 blogger_orig_url: https://blog.kagesenshi.org/2009/03/buildoutcfg-for-zopeskel.html --- A little trick to install paster away from your system python.


[buildout]
parts = zopeskel
download-cache = downloads
download-directory = ${buildout:download-cache}
install-from-cache=false

[zopeskel]
recipe = zc.recipe.egg

eggs = ZopeSkel
entry-points = paster=paste.script.command:run
