So, due to certain circumstances, Malaysia recently hosted this annual meeting where APAC regional Fedora representatives meet to discuss on regional issues and plan for the upcoming year activities and budget.

Following is a summary outcome of the meeting.
  1. Proposal will be put to Fedora Council to approve FUDCon APAC to be conducted biannually instead of annually.
  2. In the event of no country bidding to run the following FUDCon, a country will be elected to conduct FUDCon based on country profile rather than waiting for bids.
