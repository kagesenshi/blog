--- layout: post title: 'HOWTO: Data Recovery of broken filesystem/partition using The Sleuth Kit' date: '2009-12-11T09:45:00.002+08:00' author: izhar firdaus tags: - PlanetFedora modified_time: '2011-10-07T12:39:10.104+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-28371165.post-1527429225734976464 --- For those who are looking for ways to how to undelete/recover lost files from a broken filesystem, The Sleuth Kit is one option you have. It supports extracting files from NTFS, FAT(12,16,32), EXT(2,3), UFS(1,2), ISO9660, raw, and swap disks partitions.

Discovered this tool in Fedora 12 repository last night when my dad's friend's drop by at house and overheard he asked about how to recover data from his broken NTFS external HDD.
